How can we help you today?

Find answers, tutorials, and support for your academic journey at Stellar International.

quiz Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view my semester grades? expand_more
You can view your grades by navigating to the 'Marks' tab in the sidebar. Select your current semester from the dropdown menu to see a detailed breakdown of your performance in each course, including internal assessments and final examination results.
What is the procedure for fee payment? expand_more
Navigate to the 'Fees' section to see your current outstanding balance. You can pay online using our secure payment gateway (Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, or UPI). A receipt will be automatically generated and saved in your 'Payments History' section.
How do I request a change in my profile information? expand_more
Most profile fields can be updated directly from the 'Profile' section under 'Edit Profile'. However, for critical changes like Name or Date of Birth, you must submit a formal request via the 'Communication' tab along with supporting legal documentation.
Where can I find the attendance shortage warning? expand_more
Attendance details are available in the 'Attendance' tab. If your attendance falls below the mandatory 75% threshold, a red indicator will appear next to the specific subject, and you will receive an automated notification in the portal.
